The way it works;
Driver wears a Muse headband while driving - the gathered data using the Muse headband within the first few minutes of driving are considered to be the neutral state of the user. Whenever the brain activity changes from the neutral state to either extreme low/high, the driver is considered to be either in the state of sleepiness or distraction/anger. aMUSEment is the solution that monitors drivers' brain activities and plays 'energizing' or 'de-energizing' music to keep the driver out of sleepiness and road rage respectively. Energizing music will wake up the driver and de-energizing music will help the driver calm down.

The story behind it;
Born from an unsuccessful attempt to shell in to Tesla's onboard computer, Tweet my Tesla allows the user to control the Tesla car's functions via tweets. It also works as an in-car guestbook that passengers can sign. It allows the passenger to control the temperature, music, and the window controls. All done by bluetooth connection and a web-app on the Tesla's HMI unit.
